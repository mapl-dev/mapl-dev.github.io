Platform features

Everything a horse professional needs.

Purpose-built for the equine industry — no generic software adapted for barns. Every feature was designed with trainers, boarding facilities, and lesson programs in mind.

Set-and-forget invoicing
Configure recurring invoices once. STABLE pro generates and sends them automatically on your schedule — monthly, weekly, or per-service.
Client & horse profiles
Keep a complete record of every horse in your care — owner info, services, payment history, and notes all in one place.

Track stall assignments, turnout schedules, and boarding fees. Bill accurately for every horse, every month.
